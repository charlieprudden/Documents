    Why these numbers

    RDS is the dominant line item — Multi-AZ doubles instance cost, and the read replica adds a third instance. If RDS dominates beyond ~£250/mo, that's the signal to revisit instance sizing rather than over-engineer elsewhere.

    Fargate is cheap at this scale because it scales down at night. Worker tasks especially — queues drain in minutes, then idle.

    Aurora Serverless v2 is ~£40/mo more than RDS Multi-AZ for this profile but removes capacity-planning entirely. Worth revisiting at customer #20.

    What we deliberately don't pay for

    Kubernetes / EKS

    ~£70/mo for the control plane alone, plus ops overhead. Fargate is plenty for 10 customers.

    Multi-region

    Multi-AZ in eu-west-2 gives a 99.95% SLA. Multi-region is a 10× cost increase and an order-of-magnitude more complexity for the next 9 of a 9.

    Per-tenant database isolation

    Org-scoped queries with row-level filters are correct at this scale. Per-tenant physical databases multiply RDS spend and ops surface for no security benefit when the orchestrator already enforces the boundary.
